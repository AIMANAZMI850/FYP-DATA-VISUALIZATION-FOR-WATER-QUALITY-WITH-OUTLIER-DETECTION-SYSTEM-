Introduction

Over 70% of the Earth's surface is covered by water. Monitoring water bodies' quality can aid in preserving that water's purity. The chemical, physical, biological, and other components of water that vary with the seasons and geographical regions are referred to as its quality. Both the environment and human activity have an impact on it. Water pollution is a foremost global problem which needs ongoing evaluation and adaptation of water resource directorial principal at the level of international down to individual wells.
