Problem Statement

The most common problem with water quality is caused by raw water's high levels of magnesium and iron (iron(III)oxide) concentration. Water quality issues arise as a result of the water's hue changing from yellow to dark brown. In addition, low pH levels stress animals' systems and cause bodily harm, which makes them more susceptible to sickness and results in the death of fish. Water is still measured manually for diagnostic water quality, and a clinical measurement of the quality index is made in a lab. Most of the water quality monitoring systems still display simple data like pH, temperature etc. without any visualization. In order to improve the system,a new system is proposed and the way or concept is to display the data visualization using interaction in the interface. Another system analysis is probability plots are created by comparing visually histograms of measured values to the curve of theoretical distributions
